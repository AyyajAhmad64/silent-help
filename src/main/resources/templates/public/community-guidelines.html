Community safety

Community Guidelines

Silent Help is built for respectful student support, privacy, and accountable moderation.

Respect all usersCommunicate with empathy, patience, and basic courtesy.
No abusive languageHateful, threatening, or insulting content is not allowed.
No spamAvoid repeated posts, irrelevant replies, and promotional messages.
No fake requestsDo not create false emergency or support requests.
No illegal activitiesRequests or replies involving illegal actions are prohibited.
Protect personal detailsDo not publicly share phone numbers, addresses, or sensitive information.
Misuse has consequencesMisuse may result in account suspension or admin action.
