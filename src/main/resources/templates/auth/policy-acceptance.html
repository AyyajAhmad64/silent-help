Terms & Conditions

Use Silent Help only for genuine student support, academic collaboration, campus help, and respectful community discussion. Do not misuse anonymous posting to harass, impersonate, spam, threaten, or manipulate other users.

Requests involving money, emergency help, or sensitive personal situations may be reviewed by moderators and admins to protect users from fraud or abuse.

Privacy Policy

Your public activity may be anonymous, but account, email, enrollment, moderation, and security details are retained internally for trust, safety, audit, and abuse prevention. Admins may review reports, suspicious activity, and account history when needed.

Personal profile removal anonymizes visible identity while preserving moderation history, reports, and platform integrity records.

Community Guidelines

Be clear, kind, and practical. Share accurate help, avoid posting private data, report unsafe content, and use private chat responsibly. Fake requests, duplicate accounts, financial scams, and targeted harassment can lead to bans or review.

Internal verification and trust badges indicate admin-reviewed account confidence, not a guarantee of every interaction.
