About the platform

Anonymous help, responsible community.

Silent Help is not a mental health product. It is a college student-to-student support platform for practical academic, campus, career, financial, hostel, lost-and-found, and technical assistance.

Anonymous outside

Names stay hidden in public feeds while the database keeps accountable ownership.

Peer powered

Students can reply, share guidance, and help classmates without public exposure.

Moderated

Reports and admin tools keep abusive content under review.
